Blue Mango Deals is located on Long Island and we help small business owners connect with their customers.

Blue Mango Deals was created to help support local merchants. There are a lot of ways your local merchants can offer you deals but most of them are very expensive. Blue Mango Deals is a cost effective alternative for local merchants who want to offer their customers great deals.

We have made Blue Mango Deals easy to use. When you grab a great deal from your local merchant, you do not have to pay upfront for the deal; you pay when you redeem your voucher. So no more worrying about paying for something you may not use.
